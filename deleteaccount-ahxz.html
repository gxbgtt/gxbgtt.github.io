Account Deletion Request

Steps to Delete Account
1 Open Dark Immortals game
2 Go to "Settings" menu in the game
3 Find and click "Delete Account" option
4 Follow the on-screen instructions to confirm deletion
Important Warning & Risk Notice

Account deletion is irreversible and cannot be undone. Please read the following risks carefully:

  • All game progress, virtual items, currency and achievements will be permanently lost
  • Deleted account data cannot be recovered
  • All purchase history and game records will be erased
  • You will need to create a new account if you wish to play again

You are solely responsible for all consequences of account deletion. The developer is not liable for any losses resulting from account deletion.

Data Processing Information
Data Type Processing Method Retention Period
Game Progress Data Permanently Deleted Immediately
User Account Information Permanently Deleted Immediately

Purchase Records Legally Retained 7 years
Customer Service Records Legally Retained 3 years
Additional Information

Account deletion requests typically take 1-3 business days to process. You will not be able to log in to the game during this period.

According to legal requirements, some data (such as transaction records) must be retained for a specific period and will be automatically deleted after the retention period expires.
